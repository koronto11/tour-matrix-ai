Who We Are

TourMatrixAI is a next-generation B2B travel platform that reshapes how custom itineraries are designed and delivered across borders. Headquartered in Singapore with a key operations office in Shanghai, we bridge outbound travel agencies and verified destination management companies (DMCs) on a single, intelligent marketplace.

Our platform combines AI-powered itinerary generation with a curated global supplier network. Agencies build client-ready proposals in minutes without relying on senior planners or limited local partnerships. DMCs gain zero-cost access to inbound demand from five continents, backed by direct payment terms and end-to-end service accountability.

With our dual presence in Singapore and Shanghai, we stay close to both the innovation pulse of Southeast Asian travel markets and the operational depth of China's destination expertise—ensuring seamless connectivity between source markets and local delivery.
